Hidden Portals

Welcome, this is a page for those joining Hidden Portals with a tiger mask.

Note: due to an error, some iPhones don't yet work in stereo mode. Until this is fixed, you can view the portals by holding the phone in your hand.

At this time, you can experience Hidden Portals in several different ways:

  1. You can use the QR codes on display at the Library of Congress maskmaking workshop where you got this mask.
  2. You can visit the 5 sites around the Library of Congress, or the original sites of these communities, at the main Hidden Portals web page
  3. Links to the 5 portals will soon be shared at a private link here for those with masks only.
